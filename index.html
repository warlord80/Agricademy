A LITTLE ABOUT AGRICADEMY

TRANSFORMING AFRICAN AGRICULTURE

The agricademy is an agricultural education and knowledge organisation of excellence, focussed on delivering cutting edge, world class and relevant agriculture-related content to individuals and organizations throughout Africa. The agricademy is a member of the dragonfly africa group and consistent with the group's focus on bringing about a food value chain revolution in africa, the agricademy has the mission of supporting and enabling a "next-gen" African green revolution, by curating and delivering best-in-class content in agriculture for students, practitioners and allied professionals in the agriculture space.

OUR MANDATE

Mainstreaming integrated education and training programmes in agriculture, agroforestry ,forestry and natural resources within african descent.

OUR VISION

Quality tertiary education, research and extension in agriculture, forestry, agroforestry and other natural resources for transformation in africa

OUR MISSION

The Agricademy has the mission of supporting and enabling a "next-gen" African green revolution by curating and delivering best in class content

THE AGRICADEMY MANIFESTO

OUR PROMISE TO YOU

The agriculturalsector plays a key role in the life of an economy and is comsidered to be the foundation of our economic system. Not only does the agriculturalsector provide necessary subsistence but also provides employment opportunities to a significant portion of the African population

The increasing demand being placed on the agricultural sector requires that we step up to help grow the skills pool, reduce unemployment, promote economic development and drive improved food security in the decades to come. We will tckle these problems by revolutionising the technigand training of agriculture-related subjects in Africa.
